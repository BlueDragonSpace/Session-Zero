TTRPG?

TTRPG stands for Tabletop Role Playing Game. The most known one is Dungeons and Dragons (D&D), but there are plenty of other TTRPGs, like Pathfinder and MÖRK BORG.
Usually they involve funny looking dice sets with 7 unique dies of 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, and 20 faces.
(There's also generally a "100"-sided die, but it just looks like another 10 sided die.)


Just look at those dice...


Rules of Play


1. Create and ship anything related to TTRPGs

This can be anything from a virtual battle map, a dice roll calculator / automator, combat timer, dungeon / player / npc generator, spellbook sorter, technical cosplay, a tool to make a new TTRPG...
...or literally anything else you can think up!
If you aren't sure that your idea is close enough being TTRPG-themed, shoot me a message on Slack and I'll review it (it probably is, I'm pretty lenient)!

2. Track at least 5 hours using Hackatime and GitHub

Hackatime is Hack Club's automatic coding time tracking tool! If you'd like to use an alternative hour tracking tool, like Lapse, that'll work too!

3. Ship a finished project, get free dice!

Put your final polish on a project, then show it to the world!
We'll send a grant by HCB to buy any dice set you'd like!
